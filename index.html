About Me

I’m a computer science student with a strong focus on data science and deep learning. I enjoy working on problems where theory meets practice — especially when models actually have to work on real, messy data. I’ve spent years competing in algorithmic competitions. I’m always curious, a bit obsessive about learning, and motivated by building things that are both technically solid and genuinely useful. My strongest areas of knowledge are data structures & algorithms, and deep learning.
